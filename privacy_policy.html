Privacy Policy for Zombees Guides

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zombees Guides ("we," "our," or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Call of Duty Zombies companion application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do NOT collect, store, or process any personal data. Zombees Guides does not:

  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Access your device's contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Track your location
  • Store any user-generated content on external servers

2.2 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your guide progress and completion status
  • Your theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Selected guides and player count settings

This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage data and improve the app. This service may collect:

  • Device identifiers and device information
  • App usage patterns and screen views
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App performance and crash data

All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

3.2 Advertising

Zombees Guides displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. AdMob may collect and process certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/city level)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device type and operating system

This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. You can learn more about how Google uses advertising data at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All app data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device lock screens and encryption
  • Only downloading the app from official app stores

5. Children's Privacy

Zombees Guides is intended for users aged 13 and older, consistent with the age rating of Call of Duty games. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require age verification since no personal data is collected.

6. Internet Connection

Zombees Guides works fully offline. An internet connection is only required for:

  • Downloading and updating the app
  • Loading advertisements (if ad-supported version)

All guides, tools, and features function without an internet connection.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All app data stored locally on your device can be cleared by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing app data through your device settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Links and Content

Zombees Guides may display images and content related to Call of Duty games. We are not affiliated with Activision, Treyarch, or Call of Duty. All game-related trademarks, logos, and content are the property of their respective owners.

10. International Users

Zombees Guides is available worldwide. Since all user-generated data is stored locally on your device, data residency laws do not apply to that content. However, analytics and advertising data collected by Google (Analytics and AdMob) may be transferred and processed internationally in accordance with Google's privacy practices and applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

11. Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (which features are used, screen views)
  • General location information (country/region level)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors)
  • Session duration and frequency

Important: Google Analytics does NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiable information (names, emails, etc.)
  • Precise location data
  • Content of your guides or progress

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We use it solely to improve app functionality and user experience. Google's use of analytics data is governed by their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of analytics collection on your device through your device settings or by limiting ad tracking as described in section 3.2.

Summary: Zombees Guides respects your privacy. We don't collect personal data, everything is stored locally on your device. We use Google Analytics to improve the app and Google AdMob serves ads. All data collection is anonymous and handled by Google.
